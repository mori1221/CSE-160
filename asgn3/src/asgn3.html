Note to Grader

Dear Grader,

This is a story about a cat that is confined in a dilapidated house, waiting alone for its owner. The cat loves Tuna! Placing a tuna block by "Add Tuna" triggers a state change where the cat performs a walking animation and physically moves toward the target grid coordinate. Click "Clear Tuna" to stop feeding action. Press "Space" to create ice block, press key J to remove the ice block.
